About ART BY M Meet our artist Miriam Sarai Torres! How to find Miriam

We create stickers, mugs, shirts, and hats with unique designs. Our designs are created by our amazing artist Miriam Sarai Torres. Many of our designs/ artwork are inspired by the Mexican culture. Our artist and many of our staff are Mexican born or come from a Latino background and we are very proud of our culture, so, we have tried to incorporate these elements to some of our designs.

We hope that our clients can feel that same pride through our designs as much as we do. We also create designs using popular pop culture, but with a twist. If interested, we also create customized character designs to some of your favorite shows like Bob's Burgers, The Simpsons, South Park, etc.
